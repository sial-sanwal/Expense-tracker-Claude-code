Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

Spendly stores only the data you voluntarily enter, including expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect any personal information beyond what you provide for your expense tracking.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking features of Spendly. This includes displaying your expenses, generating category summaries, and calculating totals for selected time periods.

3. Data Storage

All your expense data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal financial information to any external servers unless you explicitly choose to share or export your data.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with any third-party services that process your personal data. The fonts loaded from Google Fonts are the only external resources, and they receive only standard technical information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will respond to any inquiries promptly.
